About us

Who We Are

Elohay Empire Ltd is a registered business entity engaged in contracting and supply of a variety of products within Kenya. We serve a variety of clients in various sectors such as: Hospitality, Institutions, Government and NonGovernmental Organizations. We consider every client a high end clientele. Our team is comprised of dedicated staff members highly coordinated and in tune with our efficient suppliers. The team is highly concerned with value and efficiency in all components of supplying our clients.

With millions of square feet of completed projects, ELOHAY EMPIRE LTD has a proven work ethic that delivers every time. We have made strides in the industry through thoroughness and diligence. Throughout each of our projects, we strive to maintain the highest standard of quality while staying on me and on budget. We endeavour to remain relevant in a continuously evolving market in order to retain our image as solution providers of a wide range of needs for a diverse clientele.
At ELOHAY EMPIRE LTD, we tailor our commercial construction services to the individual needs of our diverse clientele. By focusing specifically on your project, we make sure our commercial construction services fit both your overall needs and long-term vision. The reliable quality control and steadfast dedication of our construction services professionals produces immediate, and long-term, sustainable success where your goals and visions are realized.
This combination makes ELOHAY EMPIRE LTD the commercial building construction company of choice time and again for clients throughout Kenya. Our commitment to excellence, safety and strong partnerships is a natural extension of the tradition, integrity and values of the company. Please take a look at our commercial construction services to learn more about why we are the perfect commercial building construction company for you.
