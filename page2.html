DESIGN/BUILD

Design/Build is a method of project delivery in which one entity assumes total responsibility for both the design and construction of the project. This approach offers many advantages over the traditional (Design/Bid/Build) approach by using the same company for the design and construction.
Design/Build enables the construction process to run smoother since it is based on a “team” concept as opposed to one where the relationship between Designer and Contractor can become adversarial due to differing proprieties.
Other advantages of the Design/Build process include:

  • The owner saving significant amounts of time and money.
  • Projects can be designed and built quicker and in a more cost effective manner.
  • Budgets, procurement of materials, and scheduling can begin before the final design work and documents are completed.
  • Owners can have more input into the design and construction phase of the project.
  • Owners can have more input into the design and construction phase of the project. Timely informed construction decisions are always forthcoming.
  • Subcontractors become a member of the team early in the construction process.
